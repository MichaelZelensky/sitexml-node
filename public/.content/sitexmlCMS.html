Sitexml CMS

Basic content management system for a SiteXML based site. No backed requirements.

Usage

  1. Download or clone https://github.com/MichaelZelensky/SiteXML-CMS into your SiteXML-based site home directory
  2. Navigate to http://[your site]/cms/
  3. Optional Use your webserver's access control system to restrict access to the CMS
