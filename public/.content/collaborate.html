SiteXML needs your help! If you have some spare time, understanding of what is going on here, and, of course, THE DESIRE to help, here are the branches of work:

  • Promote. If you put a link or create a theme in your favourite forum, social group, blog or tweet a string about SiteXML!.. Well, that will be fantastic!
  • Discussion. Your ideas, positive critics, and proposals here: SiteXML forum
  • SiteXML PHP engine: SiteXML:PHP
  • Javascript Client of a website based on SiteXML: SiteXML:jsClient
  • Plugins for popular browsers which will allow to open SiteXML without client javascript
  • Apache port that will alone make SiteXML work without any scripting engines
  • Import/Export adapters for popular web publishing engines (like content management or blog systems)
